Day 1: Foot Roll & Sip

Today's Routine (2 minutes)

  1. Prep your HydroElastic: Mix 1 stick in 12-16 oz water
  2. Stand on roller: Place ball of foot on foam roller
  3. Roll slowly: 30 seconds per foot, heel to toes
  4. Sip & absorb: Drink half your HydroElastic while rolling
  5. Finish hydrating: Complete your drink post-roll
